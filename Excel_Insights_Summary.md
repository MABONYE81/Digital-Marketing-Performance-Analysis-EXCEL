# Excel Section — Key Insights (One-Pager)
**Greenside Villas | Digital Marketing & Booking Data, Jan 2025–Jun 2026**

## The 5 things that matter

1. **Facebook reach collapsed and never recovered.** 12,099 in Jan 2025 → down 97.7% the next month → averaged just 1,219/month afterward. This wasn't a slow decline, it was a cliff — worth investigating as an account/ad-spend/algorithm issue, not "organic decay."

2. **Instagram is the stronger long-term channel.** Averaged 5,929 reach/month (5x Facebook) with a higher average engagement rate (55.5% vs 44.4%). If budget or content time has to be prioritized, this is where it's paying off.

3. **Website traffic is the one clean growth story.** Steady climb from 980 to a peak of 15,400 visitors/month — no crashes, no spikes, just consistent growth. This channel is healthy; the other two are not.

4. **The real problem is the funnel, not the traffic.** Thousands of monthly visitors and reach turned into only **9 bookings and $3,198 in revenue across 18 months** — a ~1.2% booking conversion rate, and **10 of 18 months had zero bookings**. More reach won't fix this; the listing/inquiry step needs attention first.

5. **Don't trust Jan 2025 as a baseline.** Both Facebook (12,099) and Instagram (101,015) spiked that month, almost certainly from one boosted post. Using it to set growth targets will make every later month look like a failure.

## Bottom line
Traffic isn't the constraint — conversion is. Fix the booking funnel before spending more to grow reach.
